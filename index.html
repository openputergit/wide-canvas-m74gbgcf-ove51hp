Login Information Guide

Common Locations for Login Credentials

  • Check your email for registration confirmation messages
  • Contact your system administrator or IT support
  • Look in your password manager if you use one
  • Check welcome documents or onboarding materials

Lost Password?

  • Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page
  • Reset your password through email verification
  • Contact support for manual password reset

Security Tips

  • Never share your login credentials
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication when possible
  • Regularly update your passwords

Need Additional Help?

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I forgot my username, what should I do?

A: Contact your system administrator or use your email address as username if allowed.

Q: How often should I change my password?

A: It's recommended to change your password every 90 days.

Q: What makes a strong password?

A: Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Aim for at least 12 characters.
